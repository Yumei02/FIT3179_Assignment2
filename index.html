Suicide in Australia [2012 - 2021]

The Suicide is commonly happended around the world. This issue should be higly considered and resolved. People need to actively find out the reason of people to suicide.

This visualisation shows that the ranking of the suicide rate between states does not change much over year. This is because the frequency of suicide rate are changed based on the population and the development of the state. New South Wales maintain the first place of the number of suicide over the years since it has one of the most popular city which is Sydney in its state and it has the highest population which is 8,166,369 among other states.
